About Me

My name is Irvin and I am currently pursuing a postgraduate degree in Computer Science at the University of the Philippines. Before this, I completed my Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering at the University of Santo Tomas and passed the board exam to become a licensed Registered Electrical Engineer.

My career began in engineering and construction, but over time I found myself drawn to data — how it can tell a story, reveal patterns, and support smarter decisions. That curiosity led me to shift into data analytics, where I have been growing ever since.

Outside of work and school, I enjoy reading philosophy, staying fit, and spending time with my dogs. This website is my very first HTML project and I am proud of how far it has come!
